How the analysis works

The visibility intelligence report measures how AI systems respond to realistic buyer questions in your category.

1

Buyer queries are generated

The system creates a structured set of queries that reflect how real buyers search for solutions, including discovery, problem-based, and purchase-intent questions.

2

AI models answer each query

Leading AI systems such as Claude and GPT are asked each query independently using neutral prompts that simulate a real user interaction.

3

Brand visibility is measured

The report measures whether your brand appears in responses, how prominently it appears, and which competitors are recommended instead.

What this report is not

  • This is not a generic SEO audit.
  • This is not a single AI prompt experiment.
  • This is not a marketing score with no explanation.

The report analyses multiple buyer-style queries across different AI models and explains exactly how and why your brand appears or does not appear in those responses.

FAQ

How long does the report take?

Reports are generated within a few minutes. In most cases you will receive your completed analysis shortly after checkout and submission.

What is AI visibility intelligence?

AI visibility intelligence measures how artificial intelligence systems interpret and recommend companies within a category. The report analyses how models like ChatGPT and Claude respond to buyer-style queries and whether your brand appears in those responses. It also identifies the competitors that AI consistently recommends and provides guidance on how to improve your visibility in these systems.

Which AI systems are analysed?

The current report analyses responses from leading large language models including Claude and GPT-based systems. Additional AI systems such as Perplexity and Google AI search are being integrated into future versions of the analysis.

Is this only an audit?

No. The score is only one part of the product. The real value is in the analysis, competitor context, diagnostics, and strategic recommendations that show what to improve and why it matters for AEO.

Can I see a sample report first?

Yes. You can view a sample report at foundbyai.com.au/sample-report to see the format, depth of analysis, and recommendations.

What kinds of businesses can use this?

Found By AI works best for businesses where category discovery matters. That includes SaaS products, ecommerce brands, service businesses, and companies competing in markets where buyers increasingly ask AI for recommendations.

Will this change my website automatically?

No. Found By AI does not edit your website. It analyses AI visibility and gives you a strategic roadmap for improving the signals AI systems use when they describe and recommend companies.
