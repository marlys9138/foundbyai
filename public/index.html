Questions answered.

AEO is the practice of optimising a brand so it gets cited and recommended when people ask AI tools like ChatGPT or Perplexity questions about products or services. It is the new version of SEO — instead of ranking on Google, you appear in AI-generated answers.

You enter your brand URL. Found By AI runs 20 purchase-intent prompts relevant to your industry across major AI engines, checks whether your brand appears, scores you out of 20, and generates 6 content assets designed to close the visibility gaps. Everything arrives in your inbox in 60 seconds.

They are ready-to-use content pieces generated based on your audit findings — designed to help your brand get cited in future AI answers. Specific asset types vary by brand and audit results.

If you want to know where you stand right now, start with the One-Off Audit at $197. If you are actively working on your AI visibility and want to track progress over time, the Quarterly Plan at $297 per quarter gives you four audits a year. If you are an agency or want monthly reporting and a white label PDF, Monthly Pro at $97 per month is built for that.

The One-Off Audit is a single payment with no ongoing commitment. The Quarterly and Monthly Pro plans are recurring subscriptions that can be cancelled anytime.

ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude. These are the platforms where purchase-intent discovery is currently happening at scale.

Some improvements — like adding structured data and updating key pages — can influence AI recommendations within weeks. Building sustained authority takes consistent action over months. Your report prioritises the fastest-impact actions so you see early results while building long-term visibility.
